DET Residential Schools Providing Education Outdoors for P–12 Students

Canoeing • Cycling • Environmental Studies • High Ropes • Hiking • Horse Riding • Rock Climbing • Skiing • and more…

With seven DET campuses across the State, the ROSA schools provide programs for P–12 students within public education throughout Victoria. ROSA’s vision is that “all state school students participate in quality, education-based residential outdoor programs”.
